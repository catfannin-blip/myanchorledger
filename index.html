Services

What I do

Core Bookkeeping & Close Work
  • 01
    Multi-Year Reconstruction

    Three to seven years of books rebuilt from bank statements and source documents.

  • 02
    Cleanup Engagements

    Existing books that don't tie — traced, corrected, and documented.

  • 03
    Journal Entries & Adjustments

    Accruals, reclasses, fixed-asset schedules. Each entry documented for fast review.

  • 04
    Audit Support Prep

    IDR responses organized, workpapers indexed, reconciliations tied out.

  • 05
    Routine Bookkeeping & Month-End Close

    Recurring monthly closes, bank and credit card reconciliations, and overflow bookkeeping for firms needing steady support without a full-time hire.

Tax Preparation
  • 06
    Individual & Small-Business Returns

    Individual 1040s and small-business pass-throughs — Schedule C, simple 1065s, and 1120-S returns. Prepared and signed under my PTIN. IRS representation (audits, appeals, collections) is referred to a CPA or Enrolled Agent.

Systems & Implementation Support
  • 07
    ERP Implementation & Migration Support

    Accounting-side support for ERP migrations — chart of accounts mapping, historical data validation, parallel-period reconciliation, and go-live support. We handle the general ledger side. You handle the IT.

  • 08
    Payroll System Implementation Support

    GL mapping, tax liability account setup, automated journal entry configuration, and first-period reconciliation for payroll migrations. We make sure your payroll system talks correctly to your general ledger.

Project-Based Engagements
  • 09
    AR Recovery

    A scoped engagement to diagnose stuck receivables — portal issues, invoice formatting problems, contact gaps — resolve them, and rebuild the collection process so they don't get stuck again.

  • 10
    Transaction-Level Investigations

    When something doesn't tie and nobody can figure out why. Forensic-style analysis, root cause identification, and a documented resolution memo. For the balance sheet mysteries that have been sitting there for months.

  • 11
    Due Diligence Readiness

    Stress-test the balance sheet, clean up anything that won't survive scrutiny, and assemble the financial documentation that banks, investors, and lenders will request. For companies preparing to raise capital or close a significant transaction.

  • 12
    Revenue Reconciliation

    Bridge the gap between what one system shows as sold and what the other system recognizes as revenue. We build a repeatable reconciliation process so your two sources of truth stop disagreeing.

  • 13
    Year-End Close Projects

    For companies that manage their own books month-to-month but need experienced help with year-end adjusting entries before CPA handoff. A targeted, seasonal engagement to get you across the finish line clean.

What I don't take on

Current-month operational accounting — AP, AR, general payroll, or day-to-day transactional work. My focus is historical and period-close work: the books behind the books, not the ongoing operations. One exception: S-Corp shareholder payroll for reasonable-compensation purposes, available on request.

How It Works

Every engagement, four clear steps.

From scoping through documentation, you always know where things stand.

  • 01 | Scope Defined

    We start by understanding your company size, your accounting system, and exactly what work needs to happen. No vague retainers — just a clear scope you can sign off on.

  • 02 | Access Granted

    You provide access to your ERP or accounting system. We work directly inside your environment — not in a separate tool, not on a spreadsheet. Your books, cleaned in place.

  • 03 | Work Completed

    The engagement is executed: reconciliations, close process design, audit preparation, or project work — whatever was scoped.

  • 04 | Documentation Delivered

    You receive full workpapers, deliverables, and documentation your team — and your CPA — can actually use.
